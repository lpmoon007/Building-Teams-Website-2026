Our story

Companies have the power to change lives. We help you use it.

Be Legendary is a philanthropic team building company built on one belief: a company can be for-profit and for-purpose at once. For two decades our team building experts have guided teams to come together by building something real for kids who need it — so your people grow while a child’s life genuinely changes. Every one of our team building services gives back.

What we believe

Leave your shell.

Our mark is a snail that has left its shell behind — because being legendary starts with the courage to step out of what’s comfortable. That’s true for a person, a leadership team, and a whole company.

Every event we run is built on one idea: people do their best, bravest work when it’s connected to something bigger than themselves. So we connect your team to a real act of giving — and watch what happens.

Vision & mission

Why we exist.

Our vision

Create a world filled with people living up to their legendary potential.

Our mission

Mobilize people to live a legendary life through consistent actions that resonate with their core values.

Our credo: we believe in the unique, brilliantly creative power of each person and organization — and that, joined with others, it has the capacity to change the world.

Our approach

The ABCs of being legendary.

Three simple ideas sit underneath everything we do — with your team, and with the young people we work with.

Awareness

Notice the world around you — the injustices that need attention, and the causes that align with your own passions.

Belief

Know your core values, and trust that you and your organization truly have the capacity to make a difference.

Courage

Act on your beliefs through conscious, consistent effort. Small acts, repeated, are what shift the status quo.

Underneath the ABCs is a simple map: every team is somewhere between Point A and Point B, and our job is to build the shortest honest path between them. See how our approach works →

Appreciative, not deficit

We build on what’s already great.

Most team building goes looking for what’s broken — the weakness, the 5% that isn’t working — and tries to fix it. We do the opposite.

We find the strengths a group already has and build on them. If a team is operating well 95% of the time, we grow that 95% rather than dwelling on the 5%. It’s a small shift in focus with very real, compounding results — and it’s how good groups become great.

A social enterprise

For-profit and for-purpose — on purpose.

We’re a true social enterprise: philanthropy is woven into every service we offer, not bolted on. Giving back to the community can’t be separated from what we do. We’ve intentionally stayed a for-profit company to prove that business can do well by doing good — and keep impacting the world without depending on outside funding.

1:1
Our 1:1 promise

For every executive, a young leader.

For every corporate participant in a Be Legendary executive retreat, we deliver a Be Legendary workshop to a young person aged 12–15 — introducing them to the ABCs, to their own core values, and to authentic role models and everyday heroes. Changing the world two people at a time.

Our history

Two decades of helping people rediscover their best.

2003

It began as Repario

We started under the name Repario — Latin for “to rediscover or find again” — with a simple goal: help each person rediscover the very best of who they are. Corporate America became our laboratory.

2010

We became Be Legendary

We renamed the company Be Legendary and focused on sharing what we’d learned about legendary people and how to build legendary teams.

Today

500,000+ people, and counting

We’ve produced 2,000+ events for 1,500+ companies — and worked with 80% of the Fortune 500. Our roadmap has been tested against Fortune 10 executive teams. It holds up, because every one of us is human, with the same needs and the same potential.
