Privacy Policy

Last updated 19 July 2026

Building Teams (“Be Legendary,” “we,” “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect when you use buildingteams.com, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

Information you give us. When you submit a form, request a resource, subscribe, or contact us, we collect the details you provide — typically your name, work email, company, and any message.

Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we collect standard analytics data (pages viewed, approximate location, device and browser, referring source) through cookies and similar technologies.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiry and provide the information, proposal, or resource you requested.
  • To send you materials you asked for, and — if you opt in — occasional updates you can unsubscribe from at any time.
  • To understand how the site is used so we can improve it.

We do not sell your personal information.

Service providers we use

We share information only with the providers that help us run the business, under their own privacy terms — principally HubSpot (forms, CRM, and email) and Google Analytics (website analytics). We may also disclose information if required by law.

Cookies

We use cookies for analytics and to make forms work. You can control or disable cookies in your browser settings; some site features may not function without them.

Your choices and rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, and you can unsubscribe from any marketing email using the link in that email. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

Data retention

We keep your information only as long as needed to respond to you and for legitimate business or legal purposes, after which we delete or anonymize it.

Children

Our site and services are intended for businesses and adults; we do not knowingly collect information from children under 16.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time; the “last updated” date above will change when we do.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data? Reach us at 800-513-8759 or through our contact page.
