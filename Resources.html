Questions, answered

Team building, answered

How much does corporate team building cost? +

Most corporate team building is priced per participant, and a give-back build also includes the donated goods. Cost depends on group size, activity, venue and customization — we quote each event after a quick call so the price reflects exactly what you need.

How do you measure team building ROI? +

Baseline the team, tie the experience to a target outcome — service, quality, leadership, trust or change — and follow up on behavior for 30 days against that baseline, alongside participant evaluation data.

What is charity (CSR) team building? +

Charity team building develops your team while donating real goods — bikes, shoes, boards — to children in need, so the day builds the team and serves the community at once. Traditional team building builds skills alone.

What’s the best team building for a large group? +

The best large-group activities let everyone participate at the same time. Our charity bike, skateboard and shoe builds scale to 5,000 with no one sitting on the sidelines.
