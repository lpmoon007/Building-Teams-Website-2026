Questions, answered

Texas team building FAQs

What cities do you offer team building in across Texas? +

We run corporate team building across Texas, including Dallas, Fort Worth, Houston, Austin and San Antonio. Every activity is a give-back build donated to children in that community, and we travel to any venue in the state.

Do you travel anywhere in Texas? +

Yes. Our city pages show where we’re requested most, but we bring the full experience to any venue in Texas — you provide the room and we handle everything else.

How large a group can you handle in Texas? +

From 5 to 5,000 people, including conference general sessions. We’ve produced events at world-record scale, and teams we’ve guided have built more than 50,000 bikes for children since 2003.

Do Texas events still give back locally? +

Always. In every Texas city we partner with a local charity so the bikes, shoes and boards your team builds go to children in that same community.
