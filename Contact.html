Contact & booking questions

How much does corporate team building cost? +

Price depends on your group size, the activity and your city, so we quote each event individually rather than list a flat rate. Give-back builds are priced per participant and scale cleanly from small teams to thousands. Tell us your group and we’ll send a tailored proposal — usually within a business day. For how pricing works in detail, see our team building cost guide.

How small or large can groups be? +

Anywhere from a leadership team of 5 to a full-company event of 5,000. The facilitation and logistics scale to fit — a small executive session and a stadium-sized build use the same measurable approach, just at different scale.

How far in advance should we book? +

Three to six weeks is comfortable for most events; larger or peak-season dates (spring and December fill fast) benefit from more lead time. That said, we’ve turned around great events on shorter notice — if your date is close, call 800-513-8759 and we’ll tell you honestly what’s possible.

Do you run virtual or hybrid events? +

Yes. We run fully virtual and hybrid team building for distributed teams, including remote give-back builds where the donated goods ship to kids between sessions.

Do you travel to us?

Yes — we produce events nationwide and bring the team, materials and facilitators to your venue. You pick where and when; we handle the rest.
