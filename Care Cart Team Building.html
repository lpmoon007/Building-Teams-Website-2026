Home/Activities/Care Cart
Give-back activity · On the customer

They build a cart. A child gets the care.

Teams build and fill carts with goodies — working hard without quite knowing why, or for whom. Then the recipients are revealed: children in a hospital ward. It turns a good team building day into one people never forget.

What it is

A fun, interactive give-back event — originally built for healthcare organizations — where each team builds a Care Cart and stuffs it with goodies. At the end, the “real” customers are revealed to cheers, and every cart goes to a children’s hospital ward or a local children’s organization. Giving your people a shared act of generosity lifts a standard team building day to something most have never felt.

How it works

The setup is simple — usually a hotel or meeting-center conference room. Teams assemble and stock their carts through a facilitated experience designed to inspire and motivate. The more participants, the more carts; the more carts, the more building, interaction and impact. Then comes the reveal, which reconnects everyone to the customer they serve and the difference they can make.

Who it’s built for

Like all our programs, Care Cart is tailored to your group — the curriculum flexes to the situation each of these audiences is facing:

Leadership

Senior executives & managers

Every executive event is constructed around the specific situations the team faces — and because leaders shape the whole workplace, that experience can be transformational well beyond the room.

Front line

Sales & customer service

Great salespeople and service reps can slip into tunnel vision. Care Cart re-centers them on the customer, with a memorable metaphor to carry back to the day-to-day grind.

Often overlooked

Technical service groups

Service techs often feel they’re on their own. The experience helps them see they’re part of a larger team with goals beyond what any one person does alone.

Newly formed

Cross-functional teams

Temporary teams have to build trust and open communication fast. Care Cart is an amazing kickoff — and once trust is there, creativity and productivity take off.

Program length & location

Formats run from four hours to two days, on-site or at a conference facility — wherever your group needs to be. It pairs naturally with our other give-back builds like Building A Dream bikes, shoes and skateboards, and follows the same Point A to Point B thinking.

What it costs

Care Cart flexes on group size, format, location and the goods donated, so there’s no flat rate. Tell us who your team is and what you want them to feel, and we’ll scope it with you. Book a call.

Frequently asked questions

Who receives the Care Carts? +

Each finished cart goes to a children's ward of a hospital or a local children's community organization. The recipients are revealed to participants at the end of the event — often the most moving moment of the day.

How long is a Care Cart event? +

Anywhere from a four-hour program to a two-day format. The more participants, the more carts — and the more building, interaction and impact.

Who is Care Cart best for? +

It's tailored for senior executives, managers, sales teams, customer-service teams, technical-service groups and cross-functional teams — any group that benefits from reconnecting to the customer and to each other.

Where can you run it? +

On-site, at a conference facility, or almost anywhere you need to be — the typical setup is a hotel or meeting-center conference room.
