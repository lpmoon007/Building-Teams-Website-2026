Locations

Team building, wherever your people are.

We come to you. Explore the cities we’re asked for most — or book a call for anywhere in between. Every event gives back to kids in the local community.

Since 2003, Building Teams has produced corporate team building events in cities across the United States — and the list below is simply where we’re booked most often. Wherever your meeting, conference or leadership offsite is happening, we bring the entire experience to you: the facilitators, the materials, the logistics, and the local charity partner who connects your team to the children in that community.

Every city event follows the same proven approach — an emotionally charged experience that connects heart and mind to a real business outcome, then a 30-day follow-up to confirm the change stuck. Whether you need a give-back bike build for 500 at a national sales meeting, an Ultimate Hunt through a downtown core, or an executive retreat for 12, we tailor it to your group size, venue and goals.

Questions, answered

Team building locations FAQs

What cities do you offer corporate team building in? +

We run corporate team building in 18+ cities we’re asked for most — including Dallas, Denver, Las Vegas, Los Angeles, Atlanta, Orlando, Phoenix, San Diego and St. Louis — and we travel nationwide to wherever your people are.

Do you travel to cities not on the list? +

Yes. The city pages show where we’re requested most, but we bring the full experience anywhere in the country — from a 5-person leadership retreat to a 5,000-person general session.

Do local events still give back to the community? +

Always. In every city we partner with a local charity so the bikes, shoes or boards your team builds go to children in that same community.

How far in advance should we book our city? +

Four to twelve weeks is ideal so we can coordinate the venue, the local charity partner and materials shipping. We’ve also turned events around faster when timelines are tight — just ask.
