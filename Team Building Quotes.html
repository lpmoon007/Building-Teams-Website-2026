Team Building Quotes

By James Carter · Updated July 2026

80+ of the best team building quotes — on teamwork, leadership, collaboration, trust and a little humor — hand-picked and correctly attributed, ready to open your next meeting, retreat or offsite.

By James Carter, founder of Building Teams — 25+ years designing team-building experiences for hundreds of leadership teams. Updated July 2026.

The right words at the right moment can change the temperature of a room. That’s why so many of the best leaders open a kickoff, a retreat or a hard conversation with a single quote — a shared line that names what a team is trying to become before the real work starts. A good team building quote isn’t decoration; it’s a two-minute prompt for reflection that frames everything that follows.

Below is a curated collection of more than 80 team building quotes, grouped by theme so you can find the one that fits your moment. Every quote here is one we could stand behind — well-known lines, attributed to the people who actually said them. Where an attribution is genuinely uncertain, we left the quote out rather than pass along a misquote. Use one to open a meeting, drop one into a deck, print one on the retreat agenda, or share the whole list with your team.

A quick note on how to use them: pick one. Read it aloud, put it on a slide, and ask a single question tied to it — “where did we do this well last quarter?” or “what would it take to be this team?” Ten quotes in a row is noise; one quote, connected to your actual agenda, is a spark.

How to use these quotes with your team

Quotes are a spark, not a strategy. A line from Helen Keller or Vince Lombardi can name what a group already feels and give people shared language for it — but real cohesion comes from doing something together. Use a quote to open the conversation, then give your team an actual shared experience to live it out. That’s where the words stop being a slide and start being who you are.
