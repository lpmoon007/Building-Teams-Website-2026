60 Seconds to Give FAQs

What is 60 Seconds to Give? +

A high-energy, game-show-style team building event. Teams race the clock through fast 60-second challenges, and every challenge a team completes triggers a real charitable donation to causes your company chooses in advance.

How many people can take part? +

From 25 to 500. It runs as a lively breakout or a full general-session game show, and scales cleanly with more teams and more rounds.

How does the giving work? +

Your company selects the causes and the donation amounts up front. Each completed challenge earns a flat donation to those causes, and the finals rounds are worth more, so the total giving builds through the night. The donations are funded through Building Teams.

Do participants need any special skill? +

None. The challenges are quick, funny and designed so anyone can jump in. Our host and crew run the whole show — you bring the room and your people.

How much does it cost? +

Pricing is set per participant and includes the charitable donations, and depends on your group size, city and the giving you choose. Tell us about your event and we’ll send a tailored proposal, usually within a business day.
