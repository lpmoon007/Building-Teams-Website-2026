Ideas & inspiration

Top team building activities in Dallas

Planning a team event in Dallas–Fort Worth? Here are the activities we recommend most — starting with our own give-back builds and the broader mix of experiences the city has to offer.

Our signature experiences are hands-on give-back builds, where your team assembles something real and donates it to children across Dallas–Fort Worth. The three most popular are bike builds (Building a Dream), skateboard builds (Get On Board) and shoe builds (Sole Purpose). Each pairs a shared challenge with a genuine act of giving, and every finished bike, board and pair of shoes goes to a local DFW child. Groups run from 5 to 5,000 people, and we bring the full production to your room — a downtown Dallas hotel, a corporate campus in Las Colinas, or a general session at the Kay Bailey Hutchison Convention Center.

Beyond the builds, we facilitate The Ultimate Hunt, a high-energy problem-solving challenge that works across Downtown, Uptown, Deep Ellum or the venue floor itself. If you want to plan around a budget or theme first, browse our free team building activities for ideas you can run yourself, or explore the full range of give-back experiences we facilitate.

Dallas is also a fantastic city for classic outings, and while these aren't activities we run ourselves, they pair well with a build or an offsite — and we're happy to build a custom program around any of them. Popular options include a scavenger hunt along the Katy Trail, a food-and-brewery crawl through Deep Ellum, outdoor team challenges at Klyde Warren Park, group cooking classes and market tours at the Dallas Farmers Market, downtown escape rooms, hands-on cooking classes, topgolf-style driving-range outings that get everyone competing, a trivia or game night in Uptown, a volunteer day supporting a North Texas nonprofit, a photo or video challenge across Downtown landmarks, and a cocktail- or coffee-making workshop to close out the day. Many teams pair a morning give-back build with one of these outings in the afternoon, turning a single day into a complete offsite. Whatever mix you choose, we can wrap a facilitated give-back experience around it so your Dallas team leaves with more than just a good time — they leave having made a real difference for kids in the community.

Dallas team building FAQs

What activities do you offer in Dallas? +

Our signature give-back activities — bike builds (Building a Dream), skateboard builds (Get On Board) and shoe builds (Sole Purpose) — for groups from 5 to 5,000. Every build is donated to children in the Dallas community.

Do you travel across the Dallas–Fort Worth metroplex? +

Yes. We bring the full experience to your location anywhere in the DFW metroplex — convention centers, downtown and suburban hotels, and corporate campuses. You provide the room; we handle everything else.

How large a group can you handle in Dallas? +

From 5 to 5,000 people, including conference general sessions. We’ve produced events at world-record scale, and teams we’ve guided have built more than 50,000 bikes for children since 2003.

How much does a Dallas team building event cost? +

Pricing depends on group size, the activity and your venue. Most programs are priced per participant with the charitable donation included. Book a call and we'll send a tailored proposal.
