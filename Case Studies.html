Case studies

Proof that teams can be legendary.

Real stories of organizations that found their strength, rallied around a purpose, and gave back — from a world-record bike build to a foundation built to end its own funder’s business.

These corporate team building case studies show what happens when an experience is designed to do more than fill an afternoon. Each one connects an emotional moment — often giving back to a child — to a real business outcome, and the results speak for themselves: a Guinness World Record bike build for 5,000 DaVita teammates, two executive turnarounds that grew companies from the brink, and national meetings that became the moment people remembered for years.

Read them to see our heart-and-mind method at every scale, then explore the experiences and programs behind the stories — or hear it straight from participants on our testimonials page.

Questions, answered

Case study FAQs

Does corporate team building actually deliver business results? +

It does when it’s designed to. Across these stories, give-back experiences drove measurable outcomes — a Guinness World Record build that united 5,000 people, executive turnarounds that grew companies from $350M toward $1B and from $3.5M to $15M, and national meetings that lifted engagement and retention.

What size groups do these case studies cover? +

Everything from small executive teams to a 5,000-person general session. Our activities are built to scale, so the same emotional impact lands whether it’s 12 leaders or thousands of employees.

Can you create a custom event like these for us? +

Yes — most of our work is custom-designed around a client’s cause, theme, venue and goals. Tell us the outcome you need to move and we’ll design an experience to match, then measure it over 30 days.
