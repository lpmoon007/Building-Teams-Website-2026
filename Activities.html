Signature activities

Every activity gives something back.

Your team doesn’t do trust falls — they build real bikes, skateboards and shoes that go to children in need. Pick the build that fits your group, from 5 people to 5,000.

Our give-back team building activities replace the forgettable afternoon with something your people actually carry home. Instead of an icebreaker, teams collaborate to build a real, road-ready product — a bicycle, a skateboard, a pair of shoes — and then meet the children who receive it. That moment is what makes the lesson stick: it connects the head and the heart, which is exactly why the experience changes behavior instead of just filling a calendar slot.

Each activity is built to scale from a 5-person leadership team to a 5,000-person general session, and every one ties to a real business outcome — trust, communication, change, customer focus — measured with our 30-day follow-up method. Questions, answered

Team building activity FAQs

What are give-back team building activities? +

Give-back team building activities have your team build something real — bikes, skateboards or shoes — that’s donated to children in need. The collaboration builds the team, and the donation gives the day lasting meaning and emotional impact.

Which activity is right for my group? +

Building a Dream (bike build) is the most popular and emotionally powerful. Get On Board (skateboards) is a fast lesson in change, Sole Purpose (shoes) suits a production-line feel, and Power of reCreation is a quick creativity reset. We’ll recommend the right fit for your size, venue and goals.

How many people can take part? +

Every activity scales from a 5-person team to a 5,000-person general session, with everyone participating at once — no one sits on the sidelines.

Can the activities run virtually? +

Yes. We run turn-key virtual versions — we ship materials to participants and facilitate every breakout room — including fully virtual bike builds like the one we ran for Lenovo.
