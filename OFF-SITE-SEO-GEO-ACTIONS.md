## GEO (AI answers) — how the above maps
There is no separate "GEO" task list — GEO is won by the same off-site work:
- **Reviews + directories + listicles** (Tiers 1–2) = what ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews cite for "best/top team building" questions.
- **Digital PR** (Tier 3) = authoritative mentions AI treats as trust signals.
- **Consistent entity data** across the web (same description, `sameAs` links) helps AI recognize Building Teams as one entity.
- Optional, if notability supports it: a **Wikidata** entry (the Guinness record helps).

**GEO check (do monthly):** ask ChatGPT / Perplexity / Google AI —
*"best charity team building companies," "how to facilitate team building,"
"holiday team building activities," "corporate team building with a Guinness record"* —
and note whether Building Teams is cited. That tells you if Tiers 1–3 are working.
