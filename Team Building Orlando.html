Questions, answered

Orlando team building FAQs

What activities do you offer in Orlando? +

Our signature give-back activities — bike builds (Building a Dream), skateboard builds (Get On Board) and shoe builds (Sole Purpose) — for groups from 5 to 5,000. Every build is donated to children in the Orlando community.

Do you work with conferences at the Orange County Convention Center? +

Yes — Orlando is one of our most-requested cities, and large conference groups are our specialty. We bring everything to your meeting space, including the Orange County Convention Center and I-Drive resorts. You provide the room; we handle the rest.

How large a group can you handle in Orlando? +

From 5 to 5,000 people, including conference general sessions. We’ve produced events at world-record scale, and teams we’ve guided have built more than 50,000 bikes for children since 2003.

How much does a Orlando team building event cost? +

Pricing depends on group size, the activity and your venue. Most programs are priced per participant with the charitable donation included. Book a call and we'll send a tailored proposal.
