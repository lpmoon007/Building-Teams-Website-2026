Questions, answered

Building a Dream FAQs

What is a bike build team building activity? +

A bike build is a hands-on team building activity where groups assemble real, road-ready bikes that are then donated to children in need. Our version, Building a Dream, ends with the children arriving onsite to receive the bikes.

How many people can do Building a Dream? +

From 10 to 5,000, including conference general sessions. It is our most-requested large-group activity and scales cleanly.

Do participants need any technical skill? +

None. Every bike comes with a clear kit and our team safety-checks each finished bike. Anyone can take part.

How long does it take? +

Most builds run 60 to 90 minutes including the reveal. We tailor the length to your agenda.

Is this a charity bike build? +

Yes. Building a Dream is a charity bike build. Every bike your team assembles is donated to a child in need, so it doubles as a build a bike for charity event and a high-energy team building challenge.

How does a charity bike build team building event work? +

A bike build team building event runs in three moves. Teams earn parts in quick challenges, follow a clear kit for the team building bike assembly, then meet the children who ride away on the finished bikes. No technical skill is needed.
