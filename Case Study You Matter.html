The hidden gem

A mid-size, 5-year-old real estate client that manages or owns thousands of units had a small motto — ‘You Matter’ — that no one paid attention to, yet everyone quietly believed.

What we did

Through our Discovery and Engagement Consulting, ‘You Matter’ became their ‘one thing’ that changes everything. We helped them make the belief real — and then accelerated it beyond what they imagined.

The results

‘You Matter’ dramatically decreased staff turnover while dramatically increasing customer retention. It became a cornerstone of who they are and how they operate — not a poster on a wall.

The ripple

Today ‘You Matter’ is transforming their business from the corporate office all the way to the children of their customers — creating legendary communities at every step.
