Step 1Before you book anything

Align with your sponsor in five questions

The offsites that go sideways are the ones booked before anyone agreed what it was for. Ten minutes with your exec sponsor on these five questions saves you weeks.

1. Why this offsite, why now?What's the real reason we're pulling people out of their week?
2. What does success look like the Monday after?One or two things that should be true when everyone's back.
3. Who is in the room — and who isn't?
4. What's off-limits? (topics, sacred cows)
5. What decision or commitment should the group leave with?A retreat without a decision is a nice lunch. Name the outcome now.
Rule of thumb: the ratio that works is roughly ⅓ content, ⅓ conversation, ⅓ connection. If your draft agenda is 90% presentations, it's a conference, not a retreat.
