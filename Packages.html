What drives your price — and you control it

We quote per participant and send a tailored proposal, so there are no hidden fees. Four things you decide move the number up or down:

Group size

A larger group lowers the per-person cost but raises the total. Anywhere from 5 to 5,000.

Activity

A bike build costs more per person than a lighter activity, because the donated item costs more.

Location

Travel, venue and local logistics vary by city — you pick where.

Production level

A 5,000-person general session needs more staging, AV and crew than a 30-person breakout.

Pricing & packages, answered

How much does a Building Teams event cost? +

Events are priced per participant, with the charitable donation included, and we send a tailored proposal rather than a flat rate. Your total is driven by four things you control: group size, the activity, your location, and the production the venue needs. Share those and we'll give you an accurate number quickly.

Which package includes measurement and a post-event report? +

All of them. A 30-day behavior follow-up, participant satisfaction and a community-impact summary are standard in every package, including the entry-level Build — measurement is not an upsell. The Measured Program and The Partnership add deeper reporting like ESG-ready impact reports, pre/post surveys and ROI dashboards.

What is the difference between the three packages? +

They ladder by depth, not by whether they give back or get measured — those are in all three. The Build is one measured give-back day. The Measured Program is designed around a specific business outcome with fuller reporting. The Partnership is an ongoing, bespoke engagement with executive-grade facilitation, Team LFS diagnostics and coaching.

Can we start with one event and grow into a program? +

Yes — that's how most clients work with us. Many start with a single Build, see the 30-day results, and step up. Because measurement is built into every package, you have the data to justify the next step.

Do you have options for smaller teams or tighter budgets? +

Yes. Because pricing is per participant and you control the activity and production level, packages scale down to small teams as well as up to 5,000. Tell us your group size and budget and we'll tailor an option that still delivers a facilitated, measured, give-back experience.

Is the charitable donation included in the price? +

Yes. For give-back builds the per-participant price includes the donated goods themselves — the bikes, boards or shoes — so you fund a team experience and a community contribution in one line item.
