What are good team building activities for work? +

The best activities for work fit the moment: quick icebreakers to open a meeting, problem-solving challenges for a half-day, leadership exercises for the executive team, and give-back builds when you want lasting impact. This free library has ideas for each.

What are quick team building ideas for a meeting? +

Short icebreakers and energizers that run in under 15 minutes work well to open or reset a meeting — you’ll find a set of them in our ideas library, with no materials or budget required.

Do these team building ideas work for remote teams? +

Yes — many adapt to virtual and hybrid teams, and we also run fully facilitated virtual experiences, including turn-key virtual bike builds, when you want it done for you.
