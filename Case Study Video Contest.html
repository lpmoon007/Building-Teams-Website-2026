Engagement Campaign · Video Contest
A multi-location clientEmployee engagement

Dozens of videos. One unforgettable tricycle. Total engagement.

Updated June 2026

How a simple location-vs-location video contest turned into both a morale rocket and a diagnostic tool.

DozensEmployee videos created
ManyLocations competing
InvaluableInsight into sites needing attention
The idea

One Be Legendary client created a video contest: which location could win in one of several categories? Employees would create the videos, and everyone would vote.

The response

Incredible. Dozens of videos poured in, with many locations producing several each. The creativity was off the charts — and participation said as much as the content.

The hidden insight

The videos were amazing, but the engagement of each location gave us something invaluable: a clear read on which sites were thriving and which needed special attention. Participation became a culture diagnostic.

The one we still talk about

Our favorite: a team built a special tricycle rigged with speakers, then took turns riding up and down a jogging path. They’d pedal furiously behind a runner, blasting ‘Eye of the Tiger’ and cheering them on faster. Absolutely hysterical — and unforgettable.

“Engagement doesn’t just lift morale — it shows you exactly where your culture needs attention.”
