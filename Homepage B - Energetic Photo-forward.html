Questions, answered

The things buyers ask us most.

What is corporate team building? +

Corporate team building is a facilitated experience that strengthens trust, communication and collaboration. At Building Teams, every activity is philanthropic — your group builds bikes, skateboards or shoes for children in need, creating real emotion and lasting behavioral change.

How large a group can you handle? +

Anywhere from 5 to 5,000 people, including full conference general sessions. We produce events at world-record scale — we ran the Guinness World Record bike build for our client DaVita — and have built more than 50,000 bikes for children.

How much does a team building event cost? +

Pricing depends on group size, the activity you choose and your location. Most corporate programs are priced per participant with the charitable donation included. Book a call and we’ll send a tailored proposal.

What makes Building Teams different? +

Everything we do gives back. Your team doesn’t just bond — they change a child’s life in the same afternoon. That blend of purpose and play drives our 4.76/5 rating across 2,200 participants.
