In short

Building Teams runs corporate team building for regulated industries — healthcare, financial services and pharmaceuticals / life sciences — where audiences are senior, skeptical and compliance-bound. Our facilitated give-back builds are credible enough for clinicians, executives and scientists, involve no sensitive or regulated data, run at conference scale, and produce measurable, ESG-ready results. We’ve served leaders across these sectors, including a Guinness World Record bike build for the healthcare company DaVita.

Why regulated industries are different

The stakes are higher, and the room is harder.

Teams in healthcare, finance and life sciences don’t respond to generic team building — and the logistics won’t tolerate improvisation. Here’s what we’re built for.

Questions, answered

Regulated-industry FAQs

Do you have experience with regulated industries like healthcare and finance? +

Yes. We’ve run events for leaders across healthcare, financial services and pharmaceuticals / life sciences, including a Guinness World Record bike build for the healthcare company DaVita. These sectors share the traits our approach is built for: senior, skeptical audiences, high stakes, strict logistics, and a need for measurable, reportable outcomes.

Can you handle risk, insurance and logistics for a large event? +

Yes. We bring professional facilitators and crew, carry event insurance, and handle setup, materials, safety and teardown. We’ve produced events at world-record scale (5,000 at once), so multi-thousand-person conferences with tight logistics are within our normal range.

Is this appropriate for skeptical, senior or highly technical audiences? +

It’s designed for them. Clinicians, financial executives and scientists have sat through every trust fall. Our give-back builds work because they’re real — the team makes something tangible for children in need — which lands with senior and technical audiences precisely because it isn’t cheesy or infantilizing.

Can you work within our compliance and privacy requirements? +

Yes. The activities don’t require any sensitive or regulated data — no patient, client or financial information is involved — so they sit comfortably alongside HIPAA-, SOC- or FINRA-conscious environments. We work within your venue, security and vendor policies, and provide the documentation and insurance certificates your procurement and risk teams require.

Does it support our ESG or CSR reporting? +

Yes — a particular fit for regulated industries. Every event gives back, and we provide ESG-ready impact reporting: items donated, beneficiaries reached and volunteer hours, in a format you can use in your sustainability and community-impact disclosures.
