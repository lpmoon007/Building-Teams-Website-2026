Signature events

The big, high-energy days

Full-day and half-day experiences that test skill, knowledge and teamwork — and almost always give something back to the community around you.

Signature · Full day · 5–5,000

Crazy Day

A high-energy full day that starts early with simple instructions, then runs your team through a rotating series of skill, knowledge and teamwork challenges — with real downtime built in to relax and bond. Fully themed to your venue or city, and limited only by your goals.

Questions, answered

Team building experience FAQs

What types of team building experiences do you offer? +

Everything from a full-day Crazy Day or an adventure-race Ultimate Hunt to give-back bike, skateboard and shoe builds, corporate sailing, facilitated sessions and large conference events — for groups of 5 to 5,000, in person or virtual.

Do you run virtual or hybrid team building? +

Yes. We’ve designed emotionally charged virtual experiences since 2003 — pre-planned formats like Count Your Blessings and Get On Board, plus fully custom builds. They’re 100% turn-key: we ship any materials to participants and facilitate every breakout room.

Are these just feel-good events? +

No. Every experience connects heart and mind to a real business outcome — customer service, quality, leadership, trust or change — and we follow up on behavior for 30 days to measure how well it sticks. People leave having genuinely learned something, not just enjoyed a day.

How large a group can you handle? +

From an executive team of 5 to a general session of 5,000. Our activities are built to scale, so everyone takes part at once — no one sits on the sidelines.

How far in advance should we book? +

Four to twelve weeks is ideal so we can tailor the experience, coordinate charity partners and ship materials. We’ve also turned events around much faster — just ask.
