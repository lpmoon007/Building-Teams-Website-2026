Questions, answered

Wenatchee team building FAQs

What activities do you offer in Wenatchee? +

Our signature give-back activities — bike builds (Building a Dream), skateboard builds (Get On Board) and shoe builds (Sole Purpose) — for groups from 5 to 5,000. Every build is donated to children in the Wenatchee community.

Do you travel to Wenatchee and North Central Washington? +

Yes. We bring the full experience to your location in Wenatchee and the surrounding region — the convention center, hotels and corporate retreats. You provide the room; we handle the rest.

How large a group can you handle in Wenatchee? +

From 5 to 5,000 people, including conference general sessions. We’ve produced events at world-record scale, and teams we’ve guided have built more than 50,000 bikes for children since 2003.

How much does a Wenatchee team building event cost? +

Pricing depends on group size, the activity and your venue. Most programs are priced per participant with the charitable donation included. Book a call and we'll send a tailored proposal.
