See it in action

Photos hint at it. Video shows you.

Press play on a real Building Teams event and you’ll get it instantly — the focus, the laughter, and the moment the room realizes what they’ve built. This is what your team’s day looks like.

What you’ll see in these videos: Real corporate teams building real things for kids in need. In our Bike Build (Building a Dream) films, teams race to assemble road-ready bikes from the frame up — then the children who receive them ride into the room for the reveal. In Sole Purpose, teams run a production line assembling durable shoes for kids who’ve never owned a pair. The Power of reCreation shows teams rediscovering creativity and donating soccer balls to youth. The “for planners” overview explains how we scale any of it from 5 people to a 5,000-person general session. Captions are available on each video on YouTube.
