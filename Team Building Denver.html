We’ve been here

We know Denver — from the Convention Center floor to the foothills.

We’ve run builds in the big halls of the Colorado Convention Center downtown, in LoDo hotel ballrooms a block off Union Station, and at corporate campuses out toward the Tech Center. Mile-High energy is real — groups here show up ready.

Questions, answered

Denver team building FAQs

What activities do you offer in Denver? +

Our signature give-back activities — bike builds (Building a Dream), skateboard builds (Get On Board) and shoe builds (Sole Purpose) — for groups from 5 to 5,000. Every build is donated to children in the Denver community.

Do you travel to venues across the Denver metro? +

Yes. We bring everything to your location anywhere in the Denver metro and the Front Range — downtown hotels, the Colorado Convention Center, corporate campuses or a mountain retreat. You provide the room; we handle the rest.

How large a group can you handle in Denver? +

From 5 to 5,000 people, including conference general sessions. We’ve produced events at world-record scale, and teams we’ve guided have built more than 50,000 bikes for children since 2003.

How much does team building in Denver cost? +

Pricing depends on group size, the activity and your venue. Most programs are priced per participant with the charitable donation included. Book a call and we'll send a tailored proposal.
