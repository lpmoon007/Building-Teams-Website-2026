The give-back where a child becomes the hero.

Your people pack care packages for someone in need — working hard, not quite knowing who it’s for. Then the children arrive as the experts to help finish the job. It turns a great team building day into a moment nobody in the room forgets.

A full ballroom on one mission — teams packing care packages, table by table, for people in need.

What it is

Operation You Matter is a philanthropic team building program built around a simple, powerful twist: your team assembles real care packages and kits for people in need, and partway through, children arrive as the “experts” to inspect, complete and receive them. The work is real, the giving is real, and the reveal is the emotional high point of the day. It runs as one of six mission themes and scales from a small team to a full conference floor.

Choose your mission

Six missions, one shared purpose.

Every mission packs care packages for a different cause. We tailor the mission and the recipient organization with you, so the day connects to something your company cares about.

How it works

From a room of strangers to a room of heroes.

1

The mission

Teams are briefed on the mission and split into small groups. Often they don’t yet know exactly who they’re packing for — which makes the reveal land harder.

2

Earn & pack

Through fast, facilitated challenges, teams earn supplies and assemble real care packages and kits — communication and collaboration kick in under a friendly clock.

3

Make it personal

Handwritten notes and decoration turn a box of goods into something human — a message from your team to a stranger who needs it.

4

The children arrive

Kids enter as the experts — to inspect the packages, guide the finishing touches, and reveal who the day was really for. This is the moment people carry home.

5

The handoff

The finished care packages are presented to the recipient organization, closing the loop on a day that gave your team something real to be proud of.

Questions, answered

Operation You Matter FAQs

What is Operation You Matter? +

A give-back team building program where your team packs care packages and kits for people in need. Partway through, children arrive as the “experts” to help complete and inspect the packages — turning a fun team activity into a moment your people never forget.

How many people can take part? +

From 5 to 5,000. It runs for a small executive team or a full conference general session, scaling cleanly — the more people, the more care packages and the bigger the impact.

What are the six missions? +

Operation You Matter runs as one of six mission themes — Deployed, Fresh Start, Comfort, Birthday, Merry and Surthrival — each packing care packages for a different cause. We tailor the mission and the recipient organization with you.

Who receives the care packages? +

Everything your team packs is donated to a vetted recipient organization chosen with you, based on the mission — from deployed service members to children and families facing a hard season. Building Teams coordinates the partnership and the handoff.

What’s included? +

Everything to run it end to end: a lead facilitator and event crew, all materials and pre-staged kits, the charitable donation of what your team packs, coordination with the recipient organization, plus full setup and teardown. You bring the room and your people; we bring the rest.

How much does it cost? +

Pricing is set per participant with the charitable donation included, and depends on your group size, city and mission. Tell us about your event and we’ll send a tailored proposal, usually within a business day.
