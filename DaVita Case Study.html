Case study
DaVitaFortune 500 · Kidney care

5,000 people. One room. A world record.

In 2017 we produced a Guinness World Record bike build for DaVita — 5,000 teammates building in one Denver room, 1,000 children waiting in another, at 7 PM on a school-night Wednesday.

5,000Teammates building at once
1,000Children in the reveal room
1Guinness World Record
2017Denver · on a school night
The challenge

DaVita wanted a record-scale moment worthy of the company’s community values — something that would unite 5,000 teammates at once and mean something far beyond the event.

The twist that raised the degree of difficulty: 1,000 children would be waiting in a separate room for the reveal — at 7 PM on a Wednesday, a school night.

What we did

We designed and produced a synchronized, room-wide bike build engineered for record-setting scale — staging, logistics, facilitation and a precisely choreographed flow so all 5,000 participants were building at once.

Then the doors opened. A thousand children streamed in to meet the teammates who had just built their bikes — a reveal timed to the second, on a school night, and worth every logistical hour.

The record

The build was verified as a Guinness World Record in 2017 in Denver — a milestone earned by DaVita and produced by Building Teams. Proof that an activity at this scale could be pulled off flawlessly, and that giving back and team building belong in the same room.

The impact

A thousand children received a brand-new bike that night. Five thousand DaVita teammates shared a single, emotional high point they’d retell for years — and a company set a world record doing good.

Also DaVita · 2005

The 2017 record wasn’t DaVita’s first legendary moment with us. Back in 2005, we produced what is still the most logistically demanding event we’ve ever run: 2,200 people across four separate rooms, each with its own speakers — all building toward a single reveal that had to land at the exact same second.

3… 2… 1 — doors open. Four rooms, four reveals, one perfectly synchronized moment.

Why it worked

Scale, logistics, and a reason to care.

An event at this scale only lands if the logistics are invisible and the purpose is real. Across both DaVita events we handled both — the production rigor to run flawlessly at record scale, and the give-back that turned a corporate session into a story.

  • ✓Record-scale production — staging, flow and facilitation for thousands at once.
  • ✓Everyone participates — no rotations, no one watching from the side.
  • ✓Built-in purpose — hundreds of kids met the teams who built their bikes.
