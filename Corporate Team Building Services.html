What does a corporate team building company do?

A corporate team building company designs and runs facilitated experiences that improve how a team works — planning, materials, staffing, facilitation and follow-up. The best ones match the format to your goal, scale from a single team to thousands, and tie the day back to real work with measurement. Building Teams does all of that — and most of what we run also produces a donated result for kids in need.

Four broad types of provider exist. Here’s where each fits — and where Building Teams sits.

Pricing

What drives the cost.

There’s no flat rate — team building services are priced to the engagement. Five things move the number:

  • ✓Group size — from a team of 5 to a general session of 5,000.
  • ✓Format — a 90-minute build vs. a multi-day offsite or an ongoing program.
  • ✓Donated product — give-back builds include the cost of the bikes, shoes or kits produced.
  • ✓Location & logistics — travel, venue, staging and materials shipping.
  • ✓Measurement & follow-up — optional baselines, 30-day tracking and reporting.

For a deeper breakdown see our corporate team building cost guide — or tell us your parameters and we’ll send a proposal.

Questions, answered

Corporate team building services FAQs

What does a corporate team building company do? +

Designs and runs facilitated experiences that improve how a team works — planning, materials, staffing, facilitation and follow-up. The best ones match the format to your goal, scale from a single team to thousands, and tie the day back to real work with measurement. Most of what we run also produces a donated result for kids in need.

How do I choose a corporate team building company? +

Match the provider to your stakes: a light activity vendor for a low-stakes social; a facilitator or full-service company when the day must change behavior; a CSR provider to add giving; an OD consultancy for multi-year transformation. Building Teams is the full-service, impact-anchored option — measurable outcomes, give-back built in, scale to 5,000. See our honest guide to choosing.

What team building services do you offer? +

Give-back and charity builds, corporate team building events, conference and large-group sessions, workshops, executive team building and the Team LFS leadership simulation, virtual and hybrid formats, team coaching, and ongoing programs — all planned, staffed and facilitated by us.

How much do corporate team building services cost? +

It varies by format, group size, location and length; give-back builds also include the cost of the donated product. Tell us your headcount, goal and budget and we’ll design something that fits — request a proposal for pricing.

What size groups can you handle? +

From a leadership team of 5 to a conference general session of 5,000. Our formats are engineered to scale so everyone participates at once — we produced the Guinness World Record bike build for DaVita with 5,000 people.

Do you offer virtual and hybrid team building services? +

Yes — fully virtual and hybrid formats, from give-back builds over video to multi-week TeamwoRx programs, with a facilitator in every breakout so remote and in-office people share one experience.
