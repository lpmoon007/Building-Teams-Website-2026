Questions, answered

Executive team coaching FAQs

What is executive team coaching? +

Coaching for the team at the top — the human side of leadership. We work on alignment, communication, trust and accountability so the people with the most leverage in your company pull in the same direction.

How is this different from leadership training? +

Training transfers knowledge; coaching changes behavior. Our work is experiential and measured — we follow up on behavior for 30 days and track visible monthly data, so leaders experience what they do, not just what they know.

What does an engagement look like? +

Anything from a single executive retreat to a year-long partnership across three velocity levels — Core, Strategic and Pinnacle — each with a dedicated Champion, monthly check-ins and quarterly alignment sessions.

Is the coaching fractional, and can it be virtual? +

Yes. It’s fractional by design — senior expertise for exactly as much as you need it — with unlimited email and phone support and virtual check-ins between in-person sessions.

How do you measure success? +

With real, demonstrable change backed by visible monthly data tied to business outcomes — not a satisfaction survey at the end of a workshop.
